My Favourite Person

My favourite person is my mom. She is one of the strongest and most supportive people I know.

She has always been there for me, encouraging me, praying for me, and helping me become the person I am today. Whether I'm doing well or struggling, she never gives up on me. Her love is constant, and her advice always helps me stay focused.

I admire her strength, her patience, and how she carries so many responsibilities with so much grace. No matter how busy she is, she always makes time for her family.

I'm truly blessed to have her in my life. She inspires me every day to work hard, stay kind, and never stop growing.

Why I Chose Her

I chose my mom as my favourite person because she's been my biggest source of love and support. No matter what I go through, she's always there, encouraging me and believing in me.


Things I Love About Her

  • She listens to me without judgment
  • She gives the best advice
  • She works hard to take care of everyone
  • She's very kind and generous
  • She makes me feel safe and loved

What She Has Taught Me

My mom has taught me to always stay humble, pray, work hard, and never compare myself with others. She's taught me that it's okay to make mistakes, what matters is that you learn from them.

I hope to make her proud one day by becoming successful, staying true to myself, and carrying her lessons with me always.
